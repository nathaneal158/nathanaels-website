Our Story

Where Europe
meets Bengaluru

Tucked into the ground floor of Brigade Signature Tower on Old Madras Road, Barley & Grapes is Bengaluru's most beloved European bistro. From slow-braised classics to handcrafted pastas and indulgent desserts, we bring the warmth of a European dining room to the heart of the city.

Our chefs obsess over every plate — sourcing the finest ingredients, building flavours with patience, and plating with an artist's eye. Whether you're sharing a lazy Sunday breakfast or celebrating a milestone, every visit is a memory crafted with care.
